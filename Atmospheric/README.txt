Energy Kiosk Observer - Revision 1 Atmospheric Sensor Humidity/Light/Temperature
=================================================================================

Design: Azrul Mohamad
Layout: Charith Amarasinghe


Boards
------

Atmospheric.brd/Atmospheric.sch - Atmospheric sensor for Revision 2 SBB
Supports accurate temperature measurement with a MCP9800
Light measured to human-eye response (Lux) by TAOS TSL2561
Relative Humidity measurement with Honeywell humidity sensor (capacitive, use PIC CTMU to measure)
